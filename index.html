Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can we start a new campaign? expand_more

We can typically launch a standard customer support or lead generation campaign within 7 to 10 business days, which includes training and system integration.

What is your pricing structure? expand_more

We offer flexible pricing models including per-hour, per-agent, or performance-based models depending on the service type and volume requirements.

Every call is recorded and a percentage is audited daily by our dedicated QA team using a comprehensive scorecard aligned with your brand standards.

Can you work with our existing CRM? expand_more

Yes, we are proficient in major CRMs like Salesforce, HubSpot, and Zendesk, and our tech team can handle most custom integrations.

While we prefer long-term partnerships for maximum impact, we offer trial periods and short-term contracts for specific seasonal projects.
